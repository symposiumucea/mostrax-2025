Vision Frames

An exciting event that combines Paper Presentation and Project Exploration, offering a platform to showcase both innovative ideas and practical solutions. Present your research, demonstrate your projects, and let your vision shape the future of technology!

Guidelines

  • Team size: Maximum of 2 participants
  • Time: 5 minutes presentation + 2 minutes Q&A
  • Topics: VLSI Design, Wireless Communication, Embedded Systems, Satellite Communication, Cryptography, Cybersecurity, AI/ML, Networks, Robotics, Drone Engineering.
  • Submission:Last date for paper submission is oct 31, the tentative selection is nov 01.
  • Certificate:Participant Certificate will be provided for participants
  • Price:Cash price for the winners

Judging Criteria

Innovation, Technical Depth, Clarity, Presentation Skills

The judge’s decision will be final and cannot be changed.

Brainy Bowl

A dynamic, multi-round challenge designed to test your knowledge of Core ECE Concepts, Coding, and General Awareness. Prepare to brainstorm, answer rapidly, and claim the title of MOSTRAX Quiz Champion!

Guidelines

  • Team size: Maximum 3 participants per team.
  • Rounds: 3 rounds in total.
  • Question Format: The quiz will consist of MCQs that can be attempted on mobile.
  • Round 1: Focuses on General Technical Questions.
  • Round 2: Focuses on IT Trends Questions.
  • Round 3: Focuses on Coding Questions.
  • Note: Bring mobile phone with internet connection.
  • Certificate will be provided for winners and runners.
  • No participants certificates will be provided.

Judging Criteria

Teams will qualify for the next round based on their scores in the previous round.

Bug Smashers

Test your debugging and coding skills in Bug Smashers! Spot and fix code errors in Round 1, then face concept-based coding questions in Round 2. Speed, accuracy, and logic will crown the ultimate bug smasher!.Through google form participants will be given option to choose either python or c programming

Guidelines

  • Team size: Maximum of 2 participants per team.
  • Rounds: 2 rounds in total.
  • Round 1: Spot the error in the given programs within given time. Selection is based on accuracy and completion time.
  • Round 2: Answer concept-based programming questions within the duration.Run the program and verify the output.
  • Certificate will be provided for winners and runners.
  • No participants certificates will be provided.

Judging Criteria

Final results will be based on performance, timing and correctness in both rounds.

Hardware Hypotheses

Showcase your technical creativity and ECE knowledge in Hardware Hypotheses! The first round tests your fundamentals through MCQs, and the top scorers will move on to the hands-on round. Here, you’ll design and implement a working circuit from scratch using given components. Prove your innovation and circuit-building skills to claim the title of the ultimate hardware enthusiast!

Guidelines

  • Team size: Maximum of 2 participants per team.
  • Rounds: 2 rounds in total.
  • Round 1: MCQs based on basic ECE concepts will be conducted in pen-and-paper mode.
  • Round 2: Shortlisted participants will apply their circuit knowledge to design, assemble, and demonstrate a circuit based on a given problem.
  • Note: Wear shoes
  • Certificate will be provided for winners and runners.
  • No participants certificates will be provided.

Judging Criteria

Final results will be based on accuracy, circuit design and practical demonstration.

IPL Auction

Step into the shoes of a franchise owner! This event tests your financial acumen, strategic planning, and cricketing knowledge. Teams will compete in a live auction across four rounds to build the most efficient 11-member squad with the minimum spend to win the title.

IPL Auction Guidelines

  • Team size: Maximum of 5 members per team.
  • Rounds: 4 rounds in total for player acquisition.
  • Player Acquisition Rounds:
    • Round 1: Buy 4 Batters
    • Round 2: Buy 1 Wicketkeeper
    • Round 3: Buy 3 All-rounders
    • Round 4: Buy 3 Bowlers
    • Total Squad Size: The final squad size after 4 rounds will be 11 members (4 Batters + 1 Wicketkeeper + 3 All-rounders + 3 Bowlers).
    • Certificate will be provided for winners and runners.
    • No participants certificates will be provided.

Winning Criteria

Winners will be judged based on the minimum total amount spent in the auction and the overall rating of the acquired team (calculated from individual player ratings).

Lie Detector

Put your observation and reasoning skills to the test! In Lie Detector, teams must spot hidden lies in statements and stories. The first round challenges you to identify the false statement among truths, while the final round dives into story-based lie detection. Think fast, stay sharp, and uncover the lies to emerge as the ultimate truth seekers!

Guidelines

  • Team size: 2 participants per team.
  • Rounds: 2 rounds in total.
  • Round 1: Questions with 3 statements each — identify the lie among them.
  • Round 2: Story-based round where teams find hidden lies in short stories within a time limit. Each correct lie earns 2 marks.
  • Certificate will be provided for winners and runners.
  • No participants certificates will be provided.

Judging Criteria

Final results will be based on performance and correctness in both rounds.

Vibe Vault

Test your Tamil music knowledge in this fun challenge! Participants will see English lines of famous Tamil songs and must translate them back into Tamil. The event has three exciting rounds, with top teams advancing each time. Show your love for Tamil songs and become the ultimate Tamil Song Master!

Guidelines

  • Team size: Maximum of 3 participants per team.
  • Rounds: 3 rounds in total.
  • Description: English lines of Tamil songs will be shown, and participants must identify them into Tamil.
  • Progression: After Round 1, shortlisted teams move to Round 2. After Round 2, selected teams advance to Round 3. The final team will be chosen by the event coordinator.
  • Note: Usage of mobile phones and internet sources is strictly prohibited.
  • Certificate will be provided for winners and runners.
  • No participants certificates will be provided.

Judging Criteria

Final results will be based on performance and correctness in both rounds.

×

Dumb C

Test your quick thinking and pop culture knowledge in Dumb C! Teams compete in guessing movies, actors, proverbs, and song lyrics under strict time limits. Sharpen your observation and memory skills to win.

Guidelines

  • Team size: Maximum of 2 participants per team.
  • Rounds: 3 rounds in total.
  • Round 1: Guess the movie name or actor's name.
  • Round 2: Guess the proverbs.
  • Round 3: Identify the song lyrics.
  • Elimination: Based on scores in each round.
  • Note: No speaking, writing, or use of mobile phones is allowed.
  • Certificate will be provided for winners and runners.
  • No participants certificates will be provided.

Judging Criteria

Final results will be based on performance and correctness in both rounds.
